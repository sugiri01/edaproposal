Understanding Our Four-Step Process

  1. Courses: Students begin with structured learning in their chosen domain (AI/ML, Computer Vision, NLP, RL, LLM, Python, or SQL). Our courses blend theoretical foundations with practical applications.
  2. Projects: Following course completion, students apply their knowledge to domain-specific projects that simulate real-world challenges and solutions. These projects are designed to reinforce learning and build a portfolio.
  3. Internships: Students are placed in internships with industry partners relevant to their domain of study. These internships provide hands-on industry experience and professional networking opportunities.
  4. Placements: Our placement process includes dedicated mentoring and interview preparation, followed by scheduling interviews with up to five potential employers, maximizing employment opportunities for graduates.
